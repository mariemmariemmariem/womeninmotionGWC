Caster Semenya

South Africa

Caster Semenya, an Olympic gold medalist in middle-distance running, has been at the forefront of the fight against discriminatory regulations targeting female athletes with differences in sexual development. She has advocated for the rights and inclusion of intersex athletes, challenging regulations that limit their participation.

Yusra Mardini

Syria

Yusra Mardini, a swimmer and refugee from Syria, has used her platform and personal story to advocate for the rights and dignity of displaced individuals. She has been a symbol of resilience and hope, highlighting the challenges faced by refugee athletes and advocating for their inclusion in global sporting events.

Asia

Li Na

China

Li Na, a former professional tennis player, has been a trailblazer for gender equality in sports, particularly in tennis. She became an outspoken advocate for athletes' rights and fair treatment, challenging the norms of Chinese sports and inspiring a new generation of female tennis players.

Mary Kom

India

Mary Kom, an Olympic boxer and multiple-time world champion, has not only achieved great success in her sport but has also been a strong advocate for women's empowerment in India. She has actively campaigned for better recognition, opportunities, and support for female boxers in the country.

Dutee Chand

India

Dutee Chand, an Indian sprinter, has been a trailblazer for LGBTQ+ rights and gender equality in sports. She successfully challenged discriminatory policies and regulations that targeted female athletes with naturally high testosterone levels, leading to changes in international sporting regulations. Her activism has brought attention to the intersection of gender, sexuality, and sports.

North America

Serena Williams

United States

Although Serena Williams represents the United States, her impact and advocacy have reached a global scale. As one of the most dominant tennis players in history, Serena has used her platform to advocate for gender equality and racial justice in sports and society. She has been an outspoken voice against discrimination and has consistently pushed for equal pay and opportunities for women in tennis.

Billie Jean King

United States

Billie Jean King, a former professional tennis player, has long been a prominent advocate for gender equality and social justice in sports. She founded the Women's Tennis Association (WTA) and has tirelessly fought for equal pay, opportunities, and recognition for female athletes.

Brandi Chastain

Europe

Nadia Comăneci

Romania

Nadia Comăneci is a Romanian gymnast who made history at the 1976 Olympics by becoming the first gymnast to score a perfect 10. Her achievements inspired a generation of gymnasts and showcased the talent and potential of women in the sport. She has been an advocate for promoting gymnastics and empowering young girls to pursue their athletic dreams.

Valentina Vezzali

Italy

Valentina Vezzali is an Italian fencer and one of the most successful female fencers in history. Off the fencing strip, she has been actively involved in promoting gender equality in sports and advocating for increased support and recognition for female athletes. Vezzali has also been a vocal supporter of social inclusion and the empowerment of women through sports.
