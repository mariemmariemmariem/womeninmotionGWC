Unveiling the Inequity: Shattering Gender Barriers in Sports

By Mariem Maatoug










Gender inequality remains a significant issue in the world of sports, where female athletes often face systemic barriers and disparities compared to their male counterparts. Despite progress in promoting gender equity, there are persistent challenges that hinder the full participation and recognition of women in sports. This article aims to explore the topic of gender inequality in sports, shed light on the key issues, and emphasize the importance of addressing these inequalities.
